About Skillera

Skillera is an AI-powered career planning and skill-building platform designed to help youth navigate the evolving job landscape with confidence and clarity.

✨ What is Skillera?

Skillera is an innovative web-based tool that leverages AI to guide individuals—especially youth and fresh graduates—through career discovery, skill gap analysis, personalized mentoring, and future-ready job prediction, tailored to cities and evolving job markets.

💡 Key Features

  • AI-Powered Job Forecasting
  • Personalized Career Mentoring
  • Skill Gap Analysis + Roadmap
  • City-Specific Youth Unemployment Insights
  • Open-source and Modular
  • Scalable for Smart Cities and Government Initiatives

🌍 Unique Value Proposition

Skillera is not just a career platform—it is a youth unemployment prevention system tailored to local economies, education systems, and evolving AI/tech-driven job demands.

🎯 Mission

To empower youth with the knowledge, tools, and guidance needed to make informed career decisions, upskill with purpose, and thrive in the job market of tomorrow.

🌟 Vision

To be the leading AI-based career intelligence platform that transforms cities by reducing youth unemployment and building skill-forward, opportunity-rich societies.

🔍 Problem It Solves

  • Lack of awareness about future career options
  • Rising youth unemployment in urban areas
  • Skill mismatch between education and market demands
  • Limited access to personalized career advice

🚀 Future Roadmap

  • Launch MVP for select cities in India
  • Partner with NGOs, government portals, and EdTech startups
  • Introduce mobile app version
  • Launch AI mentors in multiple languages
  • Integrate blockchain for certification and trust
